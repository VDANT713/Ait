About AIT India

AIT India is a platform created to share real knowledge about filmmaking, media, and the artistic journey through powerful podcast conversations with artists and creators.

Built by makers who have worked with some of the most incredible creators and helped shape their careers, AIT India now steps forward as its own face.

Our mission is to give exposure to emerging artists, decode the creative industry, and clear practical pathways for freshers entering film, media, and artistic fields.

This is not just a portfolio it is a movement for artists in transit.
